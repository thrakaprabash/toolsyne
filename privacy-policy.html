Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 17, 2025

1. No Data Collection

Toolsyne is a completely client-side application. We want to be absolutely clear:

  • We do not have user accounts or login systems
  • We do not store any personal information
  • We do not use any databases to save your data
  • We do not use tracking cookies or analytics
  • All calculations happen in your browser and are immediately discarded

2. Server Logs

Like all websites, our hosting provider may retain standard server logs including:

  • Your IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited
  • Date and time of access

These logs are automatically deleted after 30 days and are only used for maintenance and security purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We don't integrate with any third-party services that would collect your data. Our website contains no:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics trackers
  • Social media widgets
  • External CDNs (all resources are self-hosted)

4. Your Privacy

We believe privacy is a fundamental right. Our tools are designed to:

  • Work completely in your browser
  • Leave no traces of your calculations
  • Require no personal information

5. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our data practices, we'll update this policy and clearly notify users.
