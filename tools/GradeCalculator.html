How to Use Grade Calculator

  1. Select the number of graded assignments (1-10).
  2. Enter your desired course grade (0-100).
  3. Click 'Set Assignments' to adjust the number of rows.
  4. Fill in the weight (as a percentage) and grade (numeric or letter) for each assignment.
  5. Click 'Calculate Grade' to see your current grade and required score for your desired grade.
