Investor Calculator

How to Use Investor Calculator

  1. Enter the principal amount in the 'Principal Amount' field.
  2. Input the annual interest rate in the 'Interest Rate' field.
  3. Specify the investment tenure in the 'Investment Tenure' field (e.g., 5 for years or 60 for months).
  4. Select the tenure type (Months or Years) from the dropdown.
  5. Click 'Calculate Returns' to see the maturity value, total interest, and annual returns.
